Welcome to my academic website! I am Munirmahedi Paviwala, a student in economic history from India, focusing on the pre-modern South Asia.

My research interests include the intersections of economic history with social, environmental and public history.

On this website, you can find more information on my current and past research projects, as well as a selection of timelines that I have created, upholding the spirit of public history. I invite you to explore my research sections and timelines to learn more about my work and interests.

If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact me.
