These are the interactive timelines I have been working on in the spirit of public history - to bring the academic economic history to the general public. I am planning to continue to expand these and foray into more such projects.

Timelines of European Powers in India

English East India Company

This interactive timeline show the major events, milestones and information about the corporation over the course of its history, as well as how they affected both the British and Indian economies.

Portuguese In India

This interactive timeline charts major events from the arrival of Vasco da Gama to the decline of Portuguese influence in the region.

French, Danish, Ostend and Swedish Companies

This timeline traces the journey of the French, Danish, Ostend and Swedish companies in the international trade, from their establishment to their decline.

Object-oriented timelines

Chocolate-Cocoa

In this timeline of chocolate and cocoa, I have tried to trace the history of chocoloate and cocoa across continents, from antiquity.

Tea

In this timeline of tea, I have tried to bring together the global perspectives and economic perspectives for historical explorations of the tea.

Coffee

Timeline of Coffee tries to trace its origins back to the Middle East and Africa. The timeline tries to explore global connections and links of coffee with colonialism in this interactive timeline.

Broad Timeline on Indian Economic History

A broad timeline that tries to trace all the major events from antiquity in Indian Economic history. It is a work-in-progress because of its large scope. Current version focuses more on the early modern period.
